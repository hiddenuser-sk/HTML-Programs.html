AYODHYA KAND

Ayodhya Kand is a significant section in the Ramayana, an ancient Indian epic. Let me share some key points about it:

  1. Coronation Arrangements: Ayodhya Kand describes the preparations for Lord Rama's coronation as the rightful heir to the throne of Ayodhya.
  2. Rama's Exile: However, due to a twist of fate, Rama is exiled to the wilderness for fourteen years to fulfill his father's wish. His unwavering obedience and determination are evident throughout this section.
  3. Grief in Ayodhya: The Kand portrays the grief that grips not only Rama's family but the entire city of Ayodhya upon hearing of his exile.
